Frequently Asked Questions

What types of clients do you work with?

We work with EPCs, design firms, municipal utilities, industrial clients, water technology vendors, startups, scale-ups and investors navigating water infrastructure projects.

How do engagements typically work?

We offer time-boxed engagements: 60-90 day sprints for scoped deliverables and 3-6 month fixed-term contracts for ongoing support. Longer term engagements are possible depending on scope and fit. After an initial consultation, we scope the project, define clear deliverables and success metrics, set up a disciplined process and work with a weekly cadence to keep momentum.

What makes Rivium different?

Rivium is sector-specialized, boutique and founder-led. You work directly with Tejal, who brings 25 years of hands-on experience in large-scale water/wastewater projects as a CTO and technical leader. We focus on practical execution, not just strategy, with clear deliverables like gate-ready packs, pilot decision packs, and risk registers. No junior teams, no handoffs.

Where are you located?

Rivium is based in Norway and serves clients internationally. Our primary market is Europe/Nordics, but we are happy to take assignments across North America, MENA and emerging markets, depending on fit. We have the flexibility to travel or engage remotely.

How quickly can we get started?

Typically within 2 weeks of our initial conversation, depending on project scope and availability. Reach out to discuss your timeline.

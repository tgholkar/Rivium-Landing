Tejal Gholkar is the founder of Rivium Solutions, a boutique advisory firm serving the water and environmental technology ecosystem.

With 25 years of international experience spanning technical leadership, business development, and project execution, Tejal has held senior roles including Chief Technology Officer for water technology innovators. His expertise bridges engineering rigor, commercial strategy, and hands-on execution—enabling clients to move from concept to scale with confidence.

Why I do this work

I chose to serve in the water sector right out of school. 25 years later, that choice still drives me, which is why I founded Rivium Solutions. I help organizations navigate the messy middle between innovation and implementation, engineering and business impact. Rivium Solutions partners with start-ups/scale-ups on pilot-to-scale paths and established players needing strategic execution leadership: raising technology and operational readiness; prospecting, business development and new market entry; and technical/PM advisory on complex delivery.

My approach: Strategic focus, engineering clarity, disciplined execution.

Practical Coordination & Execution for Water Infrastructure Projects

Time-boxed, outcome-based support that moves water and wastewater projects from plan to execution. Serving EPCs, utilities, technology vendors, and industrial clients.

Early-Works Coordination & Project Setup

Get complex water infrastructure projects moving with clear interfaces, decision frameworks, and stakeholder alignment. We coordinate the critical early phase—permits, gate readiness, and multi-party coordination—so your project advances on schedule.

Target Clients: EPCs, design firms, utilities, municipal owners on multi-stakeholder delivery

What We Deliver:

  • Interface maps and stakeholder coordination frameworks
  • Permit and approval pathway planning
  • Gate-ready packs: risk register, decision logs, 6-week look-aheads
  • Early-works planning for tenders and EPC execution
  • Technical and project management support

Compliance & Upgrade Programs

Plan and execute wastewater treatment plant upgrades and compliance programs. We support utilities and industrial clients with tender/bid pack preparation, KPI frameworks, and technical oversight to meet regulatory requirements on time and budget.

Target Clients: Municipal utilities, industrial clients, water/wastewater asset owners

What We Deliver:

  • WWTP compliance upgrade planning and feasibility studies
  • Tender and bid pack support with clear technical specifications
  • KPI frameworks and performance monitoring systems
  • Long sewage pumping system planning
  • Technical and project management through execution

Pilot-to-Scale for Water Technologies

Accelerate water technology from pilot to commercial reference. We design rigorous pilots, validate performance, and reduce scale-up risk—helping technology vendors and startups build credibility and reach Technology Readiness Level 7.

Target Clients: Technology vendors, water tech startups/scale-ups, OEMs entering EU/Nordic markets

What We Deliver:

  • Pilot design with clear success criteria and KPIs
  • Technology readiness level advancement (TRL 4→7)
  • Validated learning cycles and root-cause analysis
  • On-time decision packs: documented baseline, verified results, costs-to-scale
  • Reference project development and case study documentation

Technical Due Diligence & Fractional Leadership

Independent technical oversight and interim leadership for investors, lenders, and growing companies. From quality control on EPC projects to fractional CTO roles, we provide the technical depth you need to make confident decisions.

Target Clients: Investors, lenders, startups, EPCs needing QC oversight

What We Deliver:

  • Technical due diligence for investors and lenders
  • Quality control (QC) on design and construction
  • Fractional CTO and interim project leadership
  • Hydraulic reviews and technical assessments (EUR 10-600M capex projects)
  • Independent verification and risk assessment
